My Dreams

20th July 2025 Clear main event, with some detached pieces

I was in a modern art exhibit. I don't know who was with me, but it was somebody who didn't understand modern art (tbf, I don't either). The gallery was inside a university. The university was a futuristic structure with white, curly, arched columns. The gallery was small, only one room with paintings, and the following two rooms that contained mockups of objects in the paintings, crafted by the students. We wandered in the rooms for some time, and then we said, "Let's get out, there is nothing here".

We went to the elevator to find out that it was 20 liras to use it. I said, "Holy crap, why is the elevator paid?". The guy paid for it, but he just threw the money on the ground, and the money disappeared. The elevator was similar to that in the book "Charlie and the Chocolate Factory". It started going down really fast, I felt myself getting lighter, but it was still not enough to lift me off the floor (around 7m/s2?). When we reached ground level, the elevator slowed down pretty softly despite its bone-shaking speed.

Then, just like the book, the elevator started moving horizontally, hovering over a dedicated gray concrete road similar to bus rapid transit lanes. The road bent to form a ramp and threw us in the air. We landed on water, some yellow floats inflated on the sides of the elevator, and it kept going. There were parts of the road made out of concrete (leveled with water). The elevator passed these patches by switching between tires and water gear. I told myself, "Whoa, the 20 liras were so worth it."

Detached pieces:

  • I was going to the cinema. Asked my math teacher if he wanted to take the students and come with me to "watch a rapidly changing array of pictures". The dream morphed into a forum page where, in a thread, people were discussing cinema using weird, non-standard terminology.
  • Something about submarines. Somebody was trying to go down, and I was preparing them and telling them to be careful. Very interesting, I wish I remembered the whole dream
  • Beer was expensive in the art gallery. I went outside to a market, checked the prices, and went back in without buying anything
19th July 2025 Blurry, fragmented main event

I was in the sea, on a medium-sized, modern, plastic-metal boat with my family. Suddenly, we started talking about magic, spells, and invisible creatures. There was a notepad with small beads on it, which moved with the words, and another one with small, yellow-spotted, black bugs constantly spawning on it.

I remember not feeling good, as the dream progressed, I started feeling more and more miserable. Then I started seeing hallucinations (yes, in a dream, very ironic). Hallucinations happened when I closed my eyes, it felt like as if I was falling into something, as if I was possessed.

My father told me a lot of people were experiencing these symptoms regularly in elevators a few years ago, and they were caused by the notepads. Notepads were placed in elevators by followers to lure people into cults. Then I cleaned some sand off myself on the seaside and got out of the sea.
